Ravenswood Lodge 777

Established in 1886, Ravenswood Lodge is a regularly constituted lodge of Freemasons working under the Grand Lodge of Illinois Ancient Free & Accepted Masons. We enjoy full fraternal relations with all regularly constituted lodges wherever so dispersed that are recognized by the Grand Lodge of Illinois AF&AM. In order to visit, you must be a Freemason and a member of a Grand Lodge recognized by the Grand Lodge of Illinois.

Our meetings are held at the Clarence P. Schwarz Masonic Temple, 6910 W. Grand Ave, Chicago, Illinois.
